SURPRISE! Tech is actually a lot more than just robots

Let's talk about Humane Technology. Ever heard of it?

Humane Technology [hu路mane tech路nol路o路gy] refers to technologies that aid humans in key human goals instead of exploiting humans for profit. In other words, when used ethically, tech can save lives in unique ways!

Okay, but why do we need it?

With unprecedented technological advancements, technology such as social media, artificial intelligence (AI), virtual reality (VR), and so many more, we have gained essential cultures, connections, and innovation. However, we live in an era where technology ethics should be considered more than ever. Technology should be developed to help us, our environment, and society rather tha harm us. Yet, as technology rapidly develops, it deploys many risks including, but not limited to:

  • Gender Bias
  • Mental Health
  • Political Polarity
  • Privacy & Safety
  • Misinformation
  • Racial Discrimination

    • It is crucial for us to consider and amplify ethical humane technology that helps our society!
